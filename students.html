STUDENTS

PROGRAM OBJECTIVES

  • Engage with students in 60 - 120 minute in person and virtual experiences.

  • Ignite students ability to move through their pain towards their destiny through storytelling, personal reflection and activities.

  • Help students recognize their own limitless potential.

TOPICS

Everything I’m Not Makes Me Everything iAM

This presentation is about Purpose. Ismail empowers students to take a hard honest look at some the most challenging experiences of their lives, helping them to gain the necessary insight required to embrace those challenges and utilize them as stepping stones. Ismail will helps students recognize how their “struggle” is preparing them for who they are destined to become.

Built Different

Ismail inspires students to realize the power of Diversity. Students connect to explore their own identities, uncover bias, learn how to leverage the diversity in their environments and discover the benefit to seeking unfamiliar perspectives.

FACTS OVER FEELINGS

In this session, Ismail helps students understand the power of Emotional Intelligence. Students learn to step outside of their feelings, to think instead of react, and to utilize their feelings as tools instead of being used by them.

G.R.O.W. Through What You Go Through

Students will leave this presentation equipped with the skills to overcome personal and academic challenges by walking through Ismail’s acronym for the word GROW.
